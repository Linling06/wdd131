Overview

Purpose

SpaceExplorer will provide an inviting introduction to astronomy for curious beginners. The site’s purpose is to explain basic astronomical concepts, showcase our solar system and other celestial objects, and encourage visitors to explore the universe through engaging narratives and interactive images.

Audience

The primary audience is anyone with an interest in space who is new to astronomy. This includes students, families, hobbyists and casual visitors seeking clear, accessible explanations and inspiring images without needing prior scientific knowledge. The site is designed to be approachable for all ages and backgrounds.

Dynamic elements

JavaScript will be used to add several interactive features. A gallery of planets and celestial objects will be generated from an array of objects. Each thumbnail will open a modal window displaying a higher–resolution image and caption; the modal can be closed by clicking the close button, clicking outside the image, or pressing the Esc key. Visitors can toggle between dark and light modes to suit their preference, and a mobile navigation menu will open and close in response to taps on a menu icon. These elements will be organized into modular functions, use event listeners for user actions, and include conditional branching to decide when to show or hide content.

Content

Home page

The home page will welcome visitors with a large hero image of the night sky or a galaxy, accompanied by the site logo and a navigation menu. Following the hero section, brief paragraphs will explain what astronomy is, why exploring the universe matters and what content the visitor will find on the site. Images will be used to illustrate planets, stars and galaxies. The home page will also include links to the gallery page and will invite users to explore further.

Gallery page

The gallery page will display a grid of planet and celestial photographs. Each thumbnail will be created from an array of objects and will open a larger image in a modal when clicked. Captions will provide the name of each planet and a short fact. Additional information such as a link back to the home page or further reading will be provided in the footer.
